About Project Athena

Project Athena is the next-generation battle royale game that will immerse you in a world where survival, strategy, and skill collide. Set in a massive 10x10 km map, you'll need to outsmart and outgun your enemies to emerge victorious. With an innovative blend of historical weapons and modern mechanics, Project Athena offers a unique twist to the battle royale genre.

The Battle Royale Experience

In Project Athena, you’ll fight to the last person standing in high-stakes 120-player matches. Whether you’re dropping in solo, teaming up with a friend in duos, or going full-force with squads, the gameplay is intense and fast-paced. Each match features a sprawling map filled with rich environments, abandoned urban areas, dense forests, and treacherous lakes that add a tactical layer to your strategy.

Weapons of the Prohibition Era

Project Athena brings a unique twist with 15 iconic weapons straight out of the prohibition era. Imagine wielding a Tommy Gun, a sawed-off shotgun, or even a classic revolver, all designed with authentic detail and perfect for close-quarter combat or long-range encounters. Each weapon offers distinct advantages, and you'll need to master them if you want to dominate the battlefield.

120 Players, Multiple Modes

The game offers various match modes, including solo, duo, and squad matches, each providing a unique experience. In solo mode, you'll rely solely on your wits and reflexes to survive. In duos or squads, teamwork becomes critical as you and your friends fight to outlast the competition. With the vast map and countless strategies to explore, every match is a fresh challenge.

Features:

  • 10x10 km Map: A massive open world to explore, hide, and fight.
  • 15 Prohibition-Era Weapons: From Tommy Guns to Molotov cocktails, an arsenal of unique weapons.
  • 120 Players: Engage in high-stakes battles with up to 120 players per match.
  • Solos, Duos, and Squads: Play the way you want with various team modes.
  • Dynamic Environments: Day-night cycles, weather effects, and destructible terrain that impact gameplay.
  • Strategic Combat: Use the environment to your advantage. Plan, set traps, and outmaneuver your enemies.

The Vision Behind Project Athena

Project Athena is more than just another battle royale game. It’s a labor of love and a bold attempt to innovate within the genre. Every aspect of the game, from the weapons to the environments, has been carefully designed to deliver a unique and memorable experience for players. Whether you’re a seasoned battle royale veteran or a newcomer, Project Athena will keep you on the edge of your seat.

About Athena

Athena is an independent solo developer with a passion for creating immersive, high-quality gaming experiences. With a background in game design and coding, Athena has always believed in the power of games to connect people and tell stories. Project Athena is the culmination of years of hard work, learning, and experimenting with cutting-edge technologies.

As a solo developer, Athena handles all aspects of the game’s creation, from programming and design to sound and marketing. The goal is to create a game that not only challenges players but also provides a unique and engaging world to explore. Athena’s commitment to excellence and player feedback ensures that Project Athena will evolve over time, bringing new content, features, and experiences to keep the community engaged.

Vision as an Independent Developer

In a world where big studios often dominate the gaming landscape, Athena’s mission is to prove that independent developers can create experiences just as compelling, if not more so. With Project Athena, Athena aims to show the world that passion, creativity, and dedication can bring about something truly special.

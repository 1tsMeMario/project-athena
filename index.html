About Project Athena

Project Athena is an upcoming first-person shooter (FPS) that combines tactical gameplay, cutting-edge graphics, and immersive storytelling. With next-gen AI and a dynamic combat system, Project Athena is set to revolutionize the FPS genre.

Fast-Paced Combat

Engage in lightning-fast gunfights with strategic mechanics that reward skill and precision.

Multiplayer Modes

Team up with friends or challenge others in competitive multiplayer modes.

Next-Gen AI

Face off against smart AI enemies that adapt to your playstyle and challenge you at every turn.

Dynamic Environments

Experience combat in destructible environments that change the way you approach each fight.

Privacy Policy

At Project Athena, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and retain your data, as well as how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your email address when you sign up for updates, newsletters, or send us a message through our website. Additionally, we may collect technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and device information when you visit our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used solely to send you game updates, newsletters, or promotional materials related to Project Athena. We may also use your technical data to improve website performance and enhance user experience. We do not share your data with third parties unless required by law.

3. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and communicate with you. If you choose to unsubscribe or no longer wish to receive communications from us, your email and any related data will be deleted from our system within a reasonable time frame.

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your user experience. Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies. You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy as necessary. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date to keep you informed about how we handle your data.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at codename.project.athea@gmail.com
